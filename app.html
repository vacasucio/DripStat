📐Rounding Rules & Dose Limits
＋
Initial Dosing
• Weight — rounded to nearest whole kg on entry
• Bolus dose — rounded to nearest 50 units
• Infusion rate — rounded to nearest 50 units/hr
• ACS max bolus — 5,000 units
• DVT max bolus — 8,000 units
• ACS max infusion — 1,000 units/hr
• DVT max infusion — 1,800 units/hr
Weight Alerts
• Weight < 30 kg — error shown, verify before proceeding
• Weight > 200 kg — warning shown, verify before proceeding
Concentration
• 25,000 units / 500 mL = 50 units/mL
• mL/hr = units/hr ÷ 50

📐Rounding Rules & Adjustment Limits
＋
PTT Adjustment Rounding
• Adjustment bolus — rounded to nearest 1,000 units
• Rate change — rounded to nearest 50 units/hr
• Rate change cap — max 100 units/hr per 1 unit/kg/hr
• Current infusion rate — enter manually or auto-filled from Initial Dosing tab
Adjustment Bolus Caps
ACS:
• PTT < 30 sec → max 4,000 units
• PTT 30–49 sec → max 2,000 units
DVT/VTE:
• PTT < 35 sec → max 5,000 units
• PTT 35–59 sec → max 3,000 units
