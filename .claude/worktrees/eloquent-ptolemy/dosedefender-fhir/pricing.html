Frequently Asked Questions
Does DoseDefender store patient data?
No. Images uploaded for Snap & Calculate are processed in real time and immediately discarded. No patient data is ever stored on our servers. We only store your email address and subscription status.
What EHRs does Snap & Calculate work with?
All of them. Snap & Calculate works with any EHR screenshot — Epic, Cerner, Meditech, Allscripts, or any other system. If it's visible on your screen, it can be extracted.
Is DoseDefender FDA cleared?
DoseDefender is a clinical reference tool for use by licensed healthcare professionals. It is not FDA cleared as a medical device. Always verify all calculations independently against your institution's current protocols.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain PRO access until the end of your billing period, then automatically revert to the free plan.
